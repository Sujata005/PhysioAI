PhysioAI

ABOUT

PhysioAI is an AI-powered platform for physiotherapy insights. This project develops an AI-powered system for posture monitoring in physiotherapy. It utilizes deep learning to analyze patient movements, detecting and correcting postural errors in real-time. The system aims to enhance rehabilitation by providing immediate feedback, improving treatment effectiveness, and potentially enabling home-based therapy. This technology represents a significant advancement in applying AI to healthcare, with the potential to revolutionize physiotherapy.

KNEE EXTENTIONS - FLEXTION

Instructions

  • Stand straight with feet shoulder-width apart
  • Slowly lower your body into a squat position
  • Keep your back straight and knees behind your toes
  • Get to the point where the angles shown in the camera is nearly 90degree and green
  • Hold for 2 seconds, then return to starting position
  • Repeat for 10-15 reps per set in about 40-50seconds
  • Perform 4 sets with 60-75seconds of rest in between sets.

Make sure you're visible in the webcam frame for better posture checks! and the angles should remain GREEN throughout the set.

ELBOW EXTENTIONS

Instructions

  • Sit or stand upright and keep your arm straight and palm upwards (supination) .
  • Slowly bend your elbow and move your arm toward shoulders till the angle of 10-20 degrees.
  • Hold for 2-3seconds for maximum flexion.
  • Then slowly move your arm towards the starting point in same range of motion nearly close to 170degrees.
  • Try to fully extend but don't lock the joint
  • Repeat for 10-15 reps per set in about 40-50seconds
  • Perform 4 sets with 60-75seconds of rest in between sets.

Make sure you're visible in the webcam frame for better posture checks! and the angles should remain GREEN throughout the set.

LATERAL RAISES

Instructions

  • Stand straight with your arms straight
  • Arms relaxed at your sides, palms facing inward (toward your body).
  • You can use light dumbbells or resistance bands, or perform it without weights during rehab.
  • Slowly lift both arms out to the sides until they reach shoulder height (parallel to the floor) till nearly close to 90 degrees till its green.
  • Hands aligned with shoulders and hold at the top for 1-2seconds as per comfort.
  • Slowly lower your arms back down to the starting position with control till close to 5-10degrees of angles.
  • Avoid swinging or dropping the arms.
  • Repeat for 10-15 reps per set in about 40-50seconds
  • Perform 4 sets with 60-75seconds of rest in between sets.

Make sure you're visible in the webcam frame for better posture checks! and the angles should remain GREEN throughout the set.
