PhysioAI

About

PhysioAI is an AI-powered platform for physiotherapy insights.his project develops an AI-powered system for posture monitoring in physiotherapy. It utilizes deep learning to analyze patient movements, detecting and correcting postural errors in real-time. The system aims to enhance rehabilitation by providing immediate feedback, improving treatment effectiveness, and potentially enabling home-based therapy. This technology represents a significant advancement in applying AI to healthcare, with the potential to revolutionize physiotherapy.

Leg Exercises

1. Step-Ups: Step up onto a low step or platform.

2. Single-Leg Stance: Stand on one leg for a few seconds, and gradually increase the duration.

3. Straight Leg Raises: Lie on your back, tighten your thigh muscle, and lift your leg a few inches off the ground.

Arm Exercises

1. Arm circles: Rotating the arm in clockwise and counterclockwise directions.

2. Cross-body arm stretch: Pulling one arm across the chest.

Shoulder Exercises

1. Shoulder Press: Stand or sit with weights at shoulder height, press them overhead until your arms are straight.

2. Lateral Raises: Stand with a dumbbell in each hand at your sides, raise your arms out to the side until they are parallel to the ground.

3. Front Raises: Stand with a dumbbell in each hand at your thighs, raise the weights in front of you to shoulder height.
